# Online Retail Sales Analysis (Power BI)
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze the online retail store’s sales data for the year 2011 to understand revenue trends, identify top-performing countries and customers, and evaluate global product demand. This analysis helps the business make informed decisions for future sales forecasting, customer targeting, and international expansion.
## Dataset used
<a href ="https://github.com/komal-3003/Data-Analysis-Dashboard1/blob/main/Online%20Retail%20Data%20Set%20(1).xlsx">Dataset </a>
## Key Business Questions / KPIs
- What is the monthly revenue trend in 2011 and are there any seasonal patterns?
- Which top 10 countries generate the highest revenue excluding the United Kingdom?
- Who are the top 10 customers contributing the most revenue?
- Which countries show the highest demand for products based on quantity sold?

## Process
- Cleaned and prepared the dataset by removing invalid transactions and filtering required data.
- Created a Revenue metric (Quantity × Unit Price).
- Performed data aggregation by month, country, and customer.
- Built visualizations to analyze revenue trends, top countries, top customers, and global demand.

## Project Insights
- Revenue shows seasonal growth with higher sales toward the end of the year.
- Several countries outside the UK generate significant revenue and product demand.
- A small group of customers contributes the majority of revenue, indicating high-value customers.

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights key sales trends, top revenue-generating countries, and valuable customers. These insights help the company improve sales forecasting, customer retention strategies, and expansion opportunities in high-demand regions.
